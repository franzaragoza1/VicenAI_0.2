BMW M4 GT3 - TECHNICAL MANUAL

=== VEHICLE SPECIFICATIONS ===

Power Unit: BMW P58 3.0L Inline-6 Twin Turbo
Chassis: Steel chassis with carbon fiber safety cell
Dimensions:
- Length: 5020mm (197.6in)
- Width: 2040mm (80.3in)
- Wheelbase: 2917mm (114.8in)
Weight:
- Dry: 1265kg (2789lbs)
- Wet with driver: 1400kg (3086lbs)
Engine:
- Displacement: 3.0L (183CID)
- Torque: 479lb-ft (650Nm)
- RPM Limit: 7200
- Power: 550bhp (410kW)

=== OPERATION ===

Shifting: Electronic paddle shift. Clutch only for start/stop
Dash: Bosch DDU 10 display
Controls: Center console switch panel (Ignition, Start, Lights, Fan)

=== DASHBOARD DISPLAYS ===

Page 1 (Race):
- RPM LED bar (Green-Red-Blue)
- Speed, Gear, Fuel Mix, Map, TC, ABS
- Tire data: Pressure and Temp

Page 2 (Qualifying):
- Focus on Lap Delta and Predictives
- Reduced engine monitoring info

Alerts:
- Low fuel, Overheating, Low Oil Pressure warnings appear on dash

=== CHASSIS & AERO ===

Suspension:
- Double wishbone (Front/Rear)
- 5-way adjustable dampers

Aero:
- Rear Wing: Adjustable angle (0-11 degrees)
- Ride Height: Affects aero balance. Rake sensitive

=== ELECTRONIC SYSTEMS ===

ABS:
- 12 positions (1-11 + Off). Lower number = Less intervention
- Tailor to grip conditions (Dry vs Wet)

Traction Control (TC):
- TC1: Main cut level
- TC2: Fine slip control
- Engine Map: Throttle maps (linear vs butterfly shapes) to suit driver preference

=== DRIVETRAIN ===

Differential:
- Adjustable Preload (Nm)
- Ramp Angles (Power/Coast): Affects locking on throttle/brake
- Clutch: Carbon, 4-plate
