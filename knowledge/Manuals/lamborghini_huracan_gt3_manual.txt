=== VEHICLE SPECIFICATIONS ===

Power Unit: 5.2L V10 Naturally Aspirated
Chassis: Aluminum/Carbon hybrid chassis
Dimensions:
- Length: 4550mm (179in)
- Width: 2050mm (80.7in)
- Wheelbase: 2620mm (103in)
Weight:
- Wet with driver: 1260kg (2778lbs)
Engine:
- Displacement: 5.2L (317CID)
- Torque: 375lb-ft (510Nm)
- RPM Limit: 8500
- Power: 500bhp (373kW) (BoP restricted)

=== OPERATION ===

Starting: Main Switch, Ignition, Start
Shifting: 6-Speed Sequential. Paddle shift. No clutch needed for gear changes.
Pit Limiter: Button on steering wheel. Dash indicates activation.
Dash Page: Rotatable switch or button to cycle pages

=== DASHBOARD DISPLAYS ===

RACE 1 & 2:
- Same data, different color schemes (Day/Night)
- Key Data: Speed, Gear, Lap Time, Fuel, Oil/Water Temps
- Alerts: Low fuel, high temp warnings overlay

QUAL 1 & 2:
- Focus on Lap Delta and Predictives
- Hides fuel economy metrics

=== CHASSIS & AERO ===

Aero Setup:
- Rear Wing: Adjustable angle. Main contributor to rear downforce
- Ride Height: Front height affects splitter efficiency significantly
- Rake: Higher rear relative to front increases oversteer and drag

Suspension:
- Springs: Corner springs stiffness controls mechanical grip
- ARB: Adjustable front/rear. Soft Front ARB = more turn-in grip
- Dampers: Compression/Rebound adjustable

=== ELECTRONICS & DRIVETRAIN ===

ABS/TC:
- ABS: 12 Position rotary. 1 = Min intervention.
- TC: 12 Position rotary. Controls wheel spin intervention

Differential:
- Preload (Nm): Adjustable. Affects locking on coast/transition
- Plate differential

Brake Bias:
- Adjustable via knob. Shifts pressure Front/Rear
