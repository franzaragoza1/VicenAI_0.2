GLOBAL MAZDA MX-5 CUP - TECHNICAL MANUAL

=== VEHICLE SPECIFICATIONS ===

Power Unit: SkyActiv-G 2.0L 4-Cylinder
Chassis: Unibody with roll cage
Dimensions:
- Length: 3911mm (154.0in)
- Width: 1727mm (68.0in)
- Wheelbase: 2308mm (90.9in)
Weight:
- Wet with driver: 1008kg (2222lbs)
Engine:
- Displacement: 2.0L (122CID)
- Torque: 151lb-ft (205Nm)
- RPM Limit: 7500
- Power: 180bhp (135kW)

=== OPERATION ===

Starting: Ignition Toggle, Start Button
Shifting: 6-Speed Sequential (SADEV). No clutch required for up/down shifts once moving.
Launch: Use clutch to start moving.
Pit Limiter: Manual activation required.

=== DASHBOARD DISPLAYS ===

MAIN PAGE:
- Display: AiM MXG 1.2
- Top Bar: RPM LEDs (Green > Red > Blue shift lights)
- Left Side: Oil Temp, Oil Press, Water Temp
- Center: Gear Indicator (Large), Speed (Below Gear)
- Right Side: Lap Time, Fuel Pressure, Voltage
- Bottom: Odometer

=== CHASSIS SETUP ===

Suspension:
- Double Wishbone (Front), Multi-Link (Rear)
- Dampers: Single adjustable DSSV (Dynamic Suspensions Spool Valve)

Ride Height:
- Adjusted via spring perch offset
- Corner Weight balancing is critical for handling

Anti-Roll Bars (ARB):
- Front: Adjustable blade stiffness
- Rear: Adjustable blade stiffness
- Disconnecting bars (asymmetric) is not typical for this car

Alignment:
- Camber: Negative camber beneficial for cornering grip
- Toe: Adjustable front and rear to tune turn-in and stability

=== DRIVETRAIN ===

Differential:
- Type: Limited Slip Differential
- Tuning: Fixed unit, no user-adjustable ramp angles or preload in this spec series

Brakes:
- Bias: Adjustable front/rear via knob in cockpit
- ABS: Not available on this car. Threshold braking required
