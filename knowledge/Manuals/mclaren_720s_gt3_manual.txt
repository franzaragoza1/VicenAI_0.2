MCLAREN 720S GT3 - TECHNICAL MANUAL

=== VEHICLE SPECIFICATIONS ===

Power Unit: M840T 4.0L V8 Twin-Turbo
Chassis: Carbon fibre MonoCage II
Dimensions:
- Length: 4664mm (183.6in)
- Width: 2040mm (80.3in)
- Wheelbase: 2696mm (106.1in)
Weight:
- Dry: 1285kg (2833lbs)
- Wet with driver: 1420kg (3130lbs)
Engine:
- Displacement: 4.0L (244CID)
- Torque: 492lb-ft (668Nm)
- RPM Limit: 8000
- Power: 520bhp (388kW) (BoP dependent)

=== OPERATION ===

Starting: Ignition Switch, Start Button
Shifting: 6-speed sequential paddle shift. No clutch needed for up/down shifts
Pit Limiter: Push button activation. Dash background changes color
Dash Page: Toggle usually on wheel (Page button)

=== DASHBOARD DISPLAYS ===

RACE PAGE:
- Shift Lights: Green > Red > Blue (Shift point)
- Key Data: Speed, Gear, Lap Time, Delta
- Systems: ABS, TC, Map positions
- Tire Data: Live pressures and temperatures

QUAL PAGE:
- Removes fuel consumption metrics
- Emphasizes predictive lap time and sector deltas

PIT LIMITER SCREEN:
- Color Coded: Green = At Limit, Red = Over Limit, Blue/Black = Under Limit

=== CHASSIS & AERO ===

Aero Setup:
- Rear Wing: 12 positions (Angle adjustments). Higher angle = More drag/downforce
- Ride Height: Front splitter sensitivity. Rake tuning essential for balance

Suspension:
- 4-way adjustable dampers (Bump/Rebound High/Low speed)
- ARB: Adjustable front and rear stiffness
- Toe/Camber: Standard alignment adjustments for contact patch optimization

=== DRIVETRAIN & BRAKES ===

Differential:
- Plate type LSD
- Preload: Adjustable (Nm). Controls locking onset
- Drive/Coast Ramps: Influence locking on throttle vs braking

ABS/TC:
- ABS: 12 positions. Tailor to grip/weather
- TC: 12 positions. Controls wheel slip allowance
- Throttle Maps: Linear, Butterfly, S-Shape options for pedal feel
