NASCAR NEXT GEN (CHEVY/FORD/TOYOTA) - TECHNICAL MANUAL

=== VEHICLE SPECIFICATIONS ===

Power Unit: 5.86L (358CID) V8 Naturally Aspirated
Chassis: Steel tube frame with safety clips
Dimensions:
- Length: 4902mm (193.0in)
- Width: 1994mm (78.5in)
- Wheelbase: 2794mm (110.0in)
Weight:
- Minimum without driver: 1383kg (3050lbs)
- Wet with driver: 1542kg (3400lbs) approx
Engine:
- Displacement: 5.86L (358CID)
- Torque: 420-550lb-ft (depending on package)
- RPM Limit: 9800
- Power: 510bhp (Short Track/RC) or 670bhp (Intermediate)

=== OPERATION ===

Starting: Ignition Switch, Starter Button
Shifting: 5-Speed Sequential Transaxle. Stick shift.
- Upshift: Pull back. No clutch needed if rev-matched/lifted
- Downshift: Push forward. Blip required
Launch: Clutch required
Pit Limiter: Dashboard lights indicate RPM range for pit speed

=== DASHBOARD DISPLAYS ===

PAGE 1:
- RPM: Digital bar/graph
- Temperatures: Water, Oil
- Pressures: Oil, Fuel
- Lap Time: Previous/Best

PAGE 2:
- Pit Speed Focus: Lights/RPM indicators emphasized
- Fuel: Pressure and voltage

PAGE 3:
- Diagnostics: Voltage, System temps

=== CHASSIS SETUP ===

Suspension:
- Independent Rear Suspension (IRS) - Major change from Gen 6
- Dampers: 4-way adjustable (Ohlins TTR)

Springs/Ride Height:
- Ride Height: Critical for aero. Measured at chassis blocks
- Shock Springs: Control vertical stiffness
- Packers/Shims: Used to fine tune height

Alignment:
- Camber: High positive LF / High negative RF (Oval setup)
- Toe: Adjustable front and rear (Rear toe steers the car)
- Skew: Rear toe adjustments affect crab-walking/skew for aero advantage

=== AERO & TIRES ===

Aero:
- Diffuser: Rear underbody diffuser sensitive to rear ride height
- Spoiler: Height determined by track package (Short/RC vs Intermediate vs Super Speedway)

Tires:
- 18-inch wheels with single center-lock lug nut
- Pressures: Critical for stagger and handling balance
